About

A short introduction — hover over the card to see the glow.

Designing at the edge of AI

I'm an AI Designer focused on making generative technologies feel natural, intuitive, and trustworthy. My work spans conversational interfaces, adaptive UI systems, and tools that help people collaborate with AI rather than just consume its output.

Previously I've worked on prompt engineering for creative tools, interaction design for LLM-powered products, and design systems built to evolve with model behaviour. I believe great AI design is invisible — the interface fades and the capability becomes a direct extension of intent.

Outside of work I experiment with generative art, write about interaction patterns for AI, and maintain a small collection of houseplants that keep my desk company.
